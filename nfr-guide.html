Field Guide · Non-Functional Requirements

The features ship the demo. The non-functional requirements decide if it survives Monday.

A working feature answers "what does it do?" Everything that decides whether it holds up in production — speed, reliability, safety, the cost of changing it later — answers "how well, under real conditions?" This guide turns those invisible qualities into questions you can ask, language you can put in a PRD, and tradeoffs you can reason about.
